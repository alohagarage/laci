Users

  • User Personas

    • The first type of user is generally eager to get a head start on their career search. They are often career-oriented and ambitious and seek concrete guidance on what steps they can take further their career. In ths case of Economics major, they may feel an element of resentment that their career options are more limited than business majors. The business program and business students may be an object of admiration or envy.
    • The second type of user may feel that they are behind the ball. May exhibit signs of despair, self-doubt. Many have not seriously considered their career path. These users may benefit from more self-discovery. Because they haven't set their mind on a career, they may be more open to a variety of options than their more driven counterparts. Grad students fall into this group of users.
